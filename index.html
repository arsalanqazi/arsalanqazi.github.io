About me

I am Arsalan Riaz, a Bioinformatician and Research Assistant at the Precision Medicine Lab and Rehman Medical Institute. My expertise lies in computational genomics, network biology, machine learning, and deep learning. I develop innovative genomic data analysis pipelines to advance precision medicine and improve healthcare outcomes.

As the data lead at an interdisciplinary cancer research lab, I focus on using network biology approaches combined with omics datasets to identify vulnerable targets in viral-induced carcinomas and dynamic molecular networks in tumor evolution. I am an active leader in the International Society of Computational Biology student council, heading the Pakistani chapter (RSG Pakistan) and serving on various committees, including Co-Chair for the Asian Student Council Symposium 2022 and Design Committee Chair for the Student Council Symposium 2023.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I have a passion for designing and 3D printing, constantly exploring new design languages and techniques. My goal is to transform complex genomic data into actionable insights and solutions, fostering collaboration and innovation within the scientific community.
