About the Workshop

Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing healthcare at an unprecedented pace. From diagnostic imaging to personalized treatment plans, AI tools are becoming essential for modern medical practice.

This intensive, hands-on workshop is designed specifically for healthcare professionals who want to understand, evaluate, and implement AI solutions in their practice. You'll gain practical experience with real AI tools and learn how to navigate the ethical and clinical considerations of AI adoption.

Our expert-led sessions combine foundational knowledge with practical application, ensuring you leave with actionable skills and a clear understanding of how AI can enhance patient care in your specific field.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know about the workshop.

Do I need prior AI knowledge to attend this workshop?
No prior AI experience is required! We start with fundamental concepts and build up to practical applications. The workshop is designed for healthcare professionals at all levels, from beginners to those with some AI exposure.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, all participants who complete the full workshop will receive an official certificate of completion. This certificate acknowledges your participation in AI for Healthcare training and can be used for continuing education credits where applicable.
What should I bring to the workshop?
Please bring a laptop (tablets or phones are not sufficient for hands-on exercises), a notebook for taking notes, and business cards for networking. All workshop materials, lunch, and refreshments will be provided.
Is the workshop content relevant for my medical specialty?
Absolutely! While we cover specific examples in areas like oncology and radiology, the AI principles and tools discussed are applicable across all medical specialties. Our speakers will address specialty-specific questions during Q&A sessions.
What happens if I can't attend after registering?
We offer full refunds up to 7 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your registration to a colleague or receive workshop materials and recordings for self-study.
Will there be networking opportunities?
Yes! The workshop includes dedicated networking time during lunch and breaks. You'll connect with fellow healthcare professionals, speakers, and AI experts in an informal setting perfect for building professional relationships.
Can I get group discounts for multiple attendees?
Yes, we offer a 10% group discount for 3 or more participants from the same institution, in addition to any early bird pricing. Contact us directly to arrange group registration and payment.
What AI tools will we actually use during the workshop?
You'll get hands-on experience with current AI diagnostic tools, medical imaging analysis platforms, and will participate in building a simple medical AI model. All tools and platforms will be provided during the workshop.
